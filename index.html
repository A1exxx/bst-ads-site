FAQ

Good questions,
straight answers.

Why do advertising robots increase sales?

Advertising robots are a powerful attention tool. Almost everyone passing by notices your offer — and that consistent visibility turns into more interest and more sales.

What kind of promotion works best?

The strongest offers are clear and genuinely advantageous for most passers-by. For example, a discount on coffee not only sells more coffee — it brings in customers who also buy pastries and other items.

What makes an advertising poster effective?

An effective poster uses minimal text — about 3–4 words — so it stays readable for both pedestrians and drivers. Avoid crowding a moving sign with information; less text means more impact.

How should the audio clip be written?

Keep it short — one or two sentences, around 10–15 seconds total. People walking past should be able to hear your offer clearly and get interested instantly.

How often should the content be changed?

We recommend refreshing your offer 1–2 times a month. It keeps customers curious and keeps your advertising performing at its best.

Do you deliver across Phuket?

Yes — we deliver advertising robots and custom signage island-wide in Phuket. Self-pickup is also available. Confirm delivery details with your personal manager.

Where can the advertising robot legally stand?

The simplest and safest placement is on your own premises — by your entrance, in your yard or parking area. For placement on public areas, local rules apply and permission may be required. Tell your manager the exact spot and we will advise on the best legal option for your location in Phuket.
